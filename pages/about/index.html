About Me

I am a data scientist with a cross-disciplinary background spanning agribusiness, economics, and applied data science. My work focuses on using data to support sound decision-making in real organizational settings—particularly where data is imperfect, constrained, or collected for purposes other than analytics.

My early professional work was rooted in agribusiness and nonprofit program settings, where I led survey design, data collection, monitoring, and reporting for donor-funded and operational projects. I worked extensively with tools such as Stata, SPSS, Access, Excel, and survey platforms like Qualtrics to manage datasets, analyze outcomes, and communicate findings to diverse stakeholders. This experience shaped how I approach data today, with a strong emphasis on data quality, consistent definitions, and the practical needs of decision-makers.

I am completing a Master of Science in Data Science with formal training in applied machine learning, databases, GIS, data engineering, and analytics ethics. I value rigor, transparency, and reproducibility, and I approach data work with an awareness of how analytical choices shape people, organizations, and systems. I am interested in roles where data is applied thoughtfully to support sound analysis, improve process, and support decision-making across real-world settings.

As analytical problems increased in scope and complexity, my work increasingly emphasized reproducible and programmatic workflows. I formalized this work through data science, combining statistical modeling, machine learning, and database-driven analysis to build end-to-end analytical systems. My projects span consumer finance, healthcare operations, data engineering, and business performance data, including natural language processing, for complaint classification data engineering for end-to-end daycare data engineering pipeline, predictive modeling for risk and default analysis, customer segmentation, time-series forecasting, and process mining of healthcare event logs for workflow and operations analysis
