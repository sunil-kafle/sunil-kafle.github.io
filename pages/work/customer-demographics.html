Bank Customers Demographics and Distribution Analysis

An interactive Tableau project analyzing bank customer demographics, balance distribution, and regional patterns to support customer segmentation and strategic insights.

Overview

Understanding customer demographics and financial distribution is essential for effective segmentation and targeted decision-making in the banking sector. This project explores how customer balance levels, age groups, gender composition, and regional patterns vary across the bank’s customer base. The goal is to provide a clear, data-driven view of customer structure to support marketing, product design, and strategic planning.

Approach

The analysis was conducted using aggregated customer data visualized through interactive Tableau dashboards. Calculated fields and parameters were used to group balances and age ranges dynamically, while geographic mapping enabled regional comparison across the UK and Ireland. Filters and controls allow users to explore demographic patterns across multiple dimensions and levels of granularity.

Results

The dashboard revealed a heavily skewed balance distribution, with a majority of customers holding lower balances and progressively fewer customers at higher balance levels. Age-based views highlighted concentration in specific working-age groups, while regional and gender breakdowns showed meaningful variation across locations. These insights support more informed customer segmentation and targeted business strategies.

Tools & Technologies

  • Tableau
  • Calculated Fields and Parameters
  • Geographic Mapping
  • Interactive Filters
