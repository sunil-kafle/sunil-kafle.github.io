Regional Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard (2018–2020)

This project analyzes state-level sales performance across the United States to support data-driven decision-making. Using interactive visual analytics, the dashboard highlights geographic patterns, regional concentration, and relative performance across states. The final output enables stakeholders to quickly identify high-performing regions, assess regional contribution, and explore sales distribution through intuitive, map-based views.

Overview

This project analyzes regional and state-level sales performance for a multi-region organization operating across the United States. Stakeholders needed a clear way to compare sales, profit, and margin across regions while identifying geographic concentration and performance trends over time. The resulting Tableau dashboard consolidates sales, profit, and category-level data into an interactive visual analytics tool that supports regional performance monitoring and strategic decision-making.

Approach

The dashboard was developed in Tableau using aggregated sales and profit data from 2018 to 2020. Multiple views were designed to support different analytical questions, including time-series analysis, regional comparison, category-level profitability, and geographic distribution. Calculated fields and table calculations were used to compute profit margin, percent change in sales, and performance thresholds. Interactive filters allowed users to explore performance by region and sales volume dynamically.

Key components included:

  • Time-series analysis of total sales and month-over-month change
  • Regional and channel-level sales breakdown
  • Sales vs. profit analysis by product category
  • State-level sales distribution using geographic mapping
  • Threshold-based highlighting for performance monitoring

Results

The dashboard revealed clear regional differences in sales and profitability, with strong concentration in a subset of states. Time-series analysis highlighted seasonal patterns and periods of volatility, while category-level views showed a strong relationship between sales volume and profit generation. The geographic map enabled rapid identification of high-performing and underperforming states, improving interpretability compared to tabular reports and supporting faster, insight-driven evaluation.

Tools & Technologies

  • Tableau
  • Calculated Fields and Table Calculations
  • Geographic Mapping (Latitude and Longitude)
  • Time-Series and Trend Analysis
  • Interactive Filters and Parameters
